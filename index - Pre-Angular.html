Advice

ViCase Tiny: When space is premium

Choose this case only when space is at an absolute premium. Examples would be wall-mount presentation screens where you mount ViCase Tiny between display and wall or under the screen. If you plan to place your ViCase behind your display on the table, consider ViCase Mini. It is taller, but you would not be able to see that anyways and for the same money you will get more options.

ViCase Mini: The default choice

This is the standard housing that gets most jobs done. With 4L it is still tiny, yet offers lots of upgradeability options. ViCase Mini supports up to i7 quad core and two 2.5" drives. More than enough for most use cases.

ViCase Ultra: The powerhouse

For the ultimate performance ViCase Ultra offers all of ViCase Mini with the following Step-Up Features:

  • CPU TDP up tp 88W
  • Low Profile PCIe Slot
  • e.g. GTX 750
  • Compatibility to ViDock Nano with VÂ²A.Card
  • RAID Disk: 6 x 2.5" HDD or 3 x 3.5" HDD
  • Silence available for Disks and GPU cards

Ideal for the most demanding workstations and servers, such as for Creative Design, Video Editing workstations, or powerful NAS or database server.

ViCool Silent

Family of Virtually Silent CPU Coolers. Large fan size that run at a constant 1200 RPM. Supersized heat exchanger does not require fan to spin up under high CPU load. At 1 m (3 feet) you can hear the sound pressure level of 15 dBA, which is equivalent to a human quietly breathing, thus making these cooler virtually silent. ViCool Silent Family is designed as a System with ViDock Mini Case. Only when used together can they achieve the extreme silent operation under full CPU load.






Pico Power Supply

Mini Computer use so called Pico Power Supplies. They do away with the huge power cables of ATX power supplies, as the power supply PCB is directly soldered to the ATX connector. Their input is 12V DC, thus you also need the AC / DC Adapter. The Pico Power Supply comes with our SATA Power Splitter for support of up to 6 drives.




AC / DC Adapter

You need the AC / DC Adapter to provide 12 V DC to the input of the Pico Power Supply. Input ranges from 100 to 240 V AC, 50 ... 60 Hz.





ViDock Expansion

ViDock SFF

ViDock SFF is a PCIe Expansion Box to add full height, full lengths, double bracket PCIe Cards to your Small Form Factor PC. GPU cards with high power consumption and large TDP (Thermal Design Power) are supported up to 6 + 8 Pin PCIe Aux Power. All PCIe cards following PCIe specifications will fit and work. ViDock requires no drivers. Driver support and Operating System compatibility is subject to the PCIe Card that you insert.




VÂ²A.Card

PCIe Add In Card to add the VÂ²C.Card connector to your PC. Compatible with full height or half height PCs, aka Low-Profile. Both bracket types are included and can be changed by you.



AC Adapter

The AC Adapter is one of three ways to provide power to your ViDock SFF. All AC / DC adapter are designed for heavy duty and will support running at 100% load 24/7. AC cable suitable for your country is included. AC Input can range from 100 ... 240 V, 50 ... 60 Hz. DC Output is 12V. DC Jack is 5.5 mm / 2.5 mm. GND on outside, +12V on inside.

You can use other power supplies with ViDock SFF as long as they are 12V, same polarity and same connector dimensions. However, do not transfer more than 120W per DC plug for safety reasons.
